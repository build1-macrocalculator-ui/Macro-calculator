Calculate your daily goal and macros

To calculate your daily goal, you first have to determine your total daily energy expenditure (TDEE). This is made up of your basal metabolic rate (BMR) and your daily activity. This determines the amount of calories you need to maintain your existing weight on a daily basis.

Step #3

Calculate your calories need to reach your fitness goals using the following

  • Weight loss = TDEE x .85
  • Maintain Weight = TDEE x 0
  • Muscle Gain = TDEE x 1.1
